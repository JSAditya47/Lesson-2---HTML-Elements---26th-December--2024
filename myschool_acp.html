Birshreshtha Noor Mohammad Public College

The Basic Informations and Facts about BNMPC

BNMPC is one of the most renowned institutions in Dhaka City
It is Situated in BGB Headquarters at Pilkhana, Dhaka.
The Instituion came into Being on 1st August, 1977
It was first named Rifles Public School later named as Birshreshtha Noor Mohammad Public College.
The Instituion has Two Versions of studying which includes Bangla Medium and English Version.
There are 2 big Playgrounds and 8 Big Buildings to execute all the important works in School.
There are around 10, 000students and around 400 Qualified Teachers There are 29 Different Co - Curricular Clubs where Students can Participate. For Example: Sports Club
